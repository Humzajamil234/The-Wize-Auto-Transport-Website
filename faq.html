Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our auto transport services

How much does car shipping cost?

Pricing depends on distance, route, vehicle size, and shipping type. We offer competitive rates with zero hidden charges. Get a free quote for accurate pricing based on your specific needs.

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

Absolutely. Every shipment includes full insurance coverage for your peace of mind. Our carriers are required to carry adequate insurance, and we can provide proof of insurance upon request.

Do I need to pay upfront?

No. You only pay once your carrier has been assigned and confirmed. We believe in transparency and only charge when we have a verified carrier ready to transport your vehicle.

Will my vehicle be tracked?

Yes. Our support team provides updates throughout the entire transport process. Many of our carriers also offer GPS tracking so you can monitor your vehicle's progress in real-time.

What's the difference between open and enclosed transport?

Open transport is the standard method where vehicles are carried on an open trailer. It's cost-effective and suitable for most vehicles. Enclosed transport provides full protection from weather and road debris, making it ideal for luxury, classic, or high-value vehicles.

Do you transport non-running vehicles?

Yes, we transport non-running vehicles. There may be an additional fee for winching services to load the vehicle onto the carrier. Please inform us if your vehicle is not operational when requesting a quote.

How far in advance should I book my shipment?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for the best carrier availability and pricing. For peak seasons (summer, holidays), booking 3-4 weeks ahead is advisable.

What preparation is needed before shipping my vehicle?

Before shipping, please: wash your vehicle for easier inspection, take photos for documentation, remove personal items, ensure the gas tank is no more than 1/4 full, disable alarms, and provide keys to the carrier.
